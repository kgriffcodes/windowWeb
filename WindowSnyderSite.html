WINDOW SNYDER

About

Window Snyder is a cyber security expert currently working as the Chief Security Officer at Square, Inc. Born to an African-American father and Kenyan-born mother, Snyder’s interest in tech brought her from her hometown of Laie, Hawaii to Boston College where she studied computer science. After graduating in the late 1990s, Snyder has proven to be a pioneer in the field of cyber security as she continues to hold high-level positions at leading tech companies, including Mozilla, Apple, and Microsoft. Snyder is also the co-author of Threat Modeling, a manual for security architecture analysis in software.

Timeline of Accomplishments

Window Snyder (born 1975) is an American computer security expert currently working as the Chief Security Officer at Square, Inc. She was one of the first computer scientists to specialize in computer security.

  • 1975: Born in Laie, Hawaii
  • 1980: Wrote her first program at 5 years old
  • 1993: Graduated college with a computer science degree
  • 2002-2005: Snyder was the senior security strategist at Microsoft.
  • 2004: Wrote her first book Threat Modeling
  • 2006-2008: Started work at Mozilla
  • 2010-2015: Worked in Privacy and Security at Apple
  • 2018-2019: Chief Security Officer and VP and GM (essentially she's running everything) at Intel
  • 2019-present: CSO at Square
